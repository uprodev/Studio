CREATIVE CUSTOM FURNITURE

designrenovatecoordinate

Looking for creative ways to organize your stuff? Custom cabinetry and furniture blends function with style. Going beyond basic bookcases, our designs shape your space, and create stylish focal points that make your home feel more complete and professionally put together..

We know that clever design can come in a variety of budgets, so we work with you to get you the end result that works best for your project.

Details, Details, Details!

We pay close attention to the details, giving your home a unique style.

Details such as: 

  • Display Lighting that adds warmth, light and focal points.
  • Materials like woods and painted finishes that combine in interesting ways.
  • Hardware, whether integrated or surface mounted, adds the finishing touch to your custom furniture design.

Shape Your Space.

Well-designed custom cabinetry and furniture transforms homes into clever and unique spaces.

We offer:

  • “Off the Wall” designs. Who says custom furniture needs to only be on a wall? We create designs that shape your space while enhancing flow and functionality.
  • Clever functionality that adds ease to your day to day lifestyle by allowing you to stay organized. 

Pulling it All Together.

Yes, we can get it done for you! We have years of experience working with a variety of preferred custom shops and vendors to bring our designs to life.

Working with the contractors and cabinetry makers, we help to coordinate the loose ends for the smoothest experience.

Not sure yet how to proceed? Our specialty is creative design, so whether you use our preferred vendors or your own, we can save you time (and headaches) in order to make the process easier from design through to completion.
