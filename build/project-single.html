Art Collectors Home on the Hudson

New York City

This meticulously renovated bachelor pad is a testament to bringing our client’s vision to life! The 2 bedroom, 2 bathroom apartment was completely redesigned, starting with floors, a full renovation of the kitchen and 2 bathrooms, as well as all of the decorative furnishings, finishes and art placement.

With a focus on accentuating the stunning Hudson River views, the living space was designed with bold, but calming, blue walls. This choice, though striking, also harmonized with the river, and served as a rich backdrop to the client’s personally curated art collection.

In the kitchen, the classic white kitchen added a bright, modern contrast to the bold blue walls. The rich, wood floors of the apartment were carried into the kitchen from the living and dining room, in order to add to the open plan feel.

The guest bedroom doubles as a versatile home office with playful pops of red and orange. With all the color throughout the apartment, we chose to go with a more neutral and peaceful palette for the primary bedroom suite. Soft warm gray colors in the bedroom were complemented by the white and black, classic nyc subway tile en suite bathroom style for a continued flow of classic elegance.
