About Robyn Singer-Dror

Robyn Singer-Dror, owner and founder of Studio D Interiors, is a published interior designer, and winner of HGTV Designer’s Challenge. Robyn is a 2nd generation interior designer, who grew up learning about the in’s and out’s of the interior design business from her father, for as long as she can remember.

Growing up she was always creative. Robyn studied fine art at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst before graduating from Parsons School of Design in Manhattan, with a BFA in Interior Design. She is drawn to understanding people and the ways that we live, and is constantly researching how to improve her clients day to day lives through smart and creative interior design.

For Robyn, it’s not only about making beautiful spaces, but it’s connecting with her clients personally, and considering all of the details that will create a home that is a more comfortable and convenient space for them to live and entertain in.

Robyn calls Riverdale, NY her home, a beautiful section of The Bronx overlooking the Hudson River. She lives there with her husband and 2 children.

Studio D Interiors

Studio D Interiors works with clients who want a full service design and project management experience. Our projects range from 1,000 sf apartment renovations through to 19,000 sf new home construction. Our team of designers and project managers allow us to complete the work you need done, within the timeline and budget agreed upon.

Our extensive experience running projects since 2001, allows us to foresee problems, prevent costly mistakes, and work efficiently with the vendors and trades, in order to bring your project to a successful completion!

We are based in the beautiful section of The Bronx called Riverdale, overlooking the Hudson River. We have projects throughout the Tri-State area, most often on Long Island, Riverdale, Manhattan and Westchester.
