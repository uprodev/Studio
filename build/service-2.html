KITCHENS & BATHROOMS

designrenovatecoordinate

Kitchens and Bathrooms are the center of our homes, where life happens! We gather, relax and unwind in these spaces, and having a well-designed kitchen or bathroom is essential to enhancing our daily lives.

We take care to properly plan your space to fit your needs, style and budget. We make suggestions based upon our extensive knowledge and experience, in order to create beautiful and functional spaces that you’ll love spending time in.

Interior Design, Not Just Cabinetry.

What sets us apart from a kitchen or bathroom showroom, is our comprehensive approach to design.  We take the time to gather information about your lifestyle, preferences, and vision for your home. 

We understand that not everyone knows exactly what they want in the beginning or the process, so we help you to clarify your vision, and develop a design that meets your needs. 

Expertise with Trade Coordination.

Need a contractor? Not sure where to purchase your cabinetry or tile? Or maybe you already have a team and need some help overseeing it all.

With over 15 years of experience in renovations and kitchen & bathroom remodeling, we coordinate your project so you have less headaches! 

Getting it done.

We provide you with the clarity and confidence you need to make informed decisions about your project.

  • 3D Interior Views. We allow you to visualize your new space before anything is built!
  • Product Selections. Appliance, fixture and tile selections based upon your specific needs and budget.
  • Detailed Plans. From concept to construction, we provide detailed plans to help the team get it done right!
