INTERIOR RENOVATIONS

designrenovatecoordinate

We offer a comprehensive interior design and renovation service for our clients, of various project sizes.  From full-scale New York City apartment renovations to private homes, we have experience and expertise to tackle any project

When needed, we offer a full range of renovation services, from interior design, contractor and trade procurement, and project management. This means that you can be confident that your project will be completed to the highest standards.

Want something smaller? Our projects also include renovations under 1,000 sf and kitchen and bathroom remodels.

Creative Solutions for your Space.

Making your space fit your style and functionality is our priority.

We help you to: 

  • Understand your Design. With our 3D Views, you see your design before anything is built, helping you to understand what your final space will look like!
  • Make Decisions. We present design options to you based on your needs and preferences, making decisions a much easier process.

Your advocate and advisor.

Renovations can be stressful. With over 15 years of experience renovating interiors, we help you through the process and make it less overwhelming.

We help you to: 

  • Simplify communication. We work with you and the team to offer clear communication during the project, and get it done!!
  • See the big picture. We provide a holistic perspective, ensuring that every aspect of your space is considered and accounted for. We help to minimize the risk of oversights and ensure that your project is completed to the highest standards.

We coordinate so you don’t have to.

We deliver beautiful, functional spaces, but also save you time (and headaches) in order to make the process as smooth as possible from design through to completion.

We help you to: 

  • Coordinate the team. We work with the team of contractors and various trades, in order to keep your project moving forward in a professional and timely manner.
